GERAR LICENÇAS

curl --ssl-no-revoke -X POST https://server-licenca-app.onrender.com/api/v1/generate_key


VERIFICAR LICENSAS

curl --ssl-no-revoke https://server-licenca-app.onrender.com/api/v1/licenses


Revogar licença

curl --ssl-no-revoke -X POST -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '{"license_key":"[SUA_CHAVE_AQUI]"}' https://server-licenca-app.onrender.com/api/v1/revoke


DELETARr licença

curl --ssl-no-revoke -X DELETE https://server-licenca-app.onrender.com/api/v1/licenses/[SUA_CHAVE_AQUI]
